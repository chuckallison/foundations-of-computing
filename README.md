## Foundations of Computing: An Accessible Introduction to Formal Languages
### by Charles D. “Chuck” Allison
#### Fresh Sources, Inc. Publisher

A clear, accessible undergraduate textbook covering formal languages, automata theory, and introductory computability.

---
📘 About the Book

Foundations of Computing is designed for undergraduate courses in:
* Formal Languages
* Automata Theory
* Theory of Computation
* Discrete Structures II
* CS Foundations for programmers

The book emphasizes clarity and intuition while maintaining mathematical rigor. It includes:
* Deterministic and nondeterministic finite automata
* Regular languages and regular grammars
* Context‑free grammars and pushdown automata
* Turing machines and decidability
* Reductions and computability
* Numerous worked examples and exercises, code examples in Python

The text is suitable for CS majors, CS minors, and motivated programmers who want a deeper understanding of computation.
